Contact

For general inquiries about dbtoaster, email dbtoaster at epfl dot ch.

For bug reports, use our bug-tracker.

Mailing Lists

  • dbtoaster-announce: A low-volume mailing list for announcements related to DBToaster.

To subscribe to the mailing list, send an email to dbtoaster-announce-subscribe@listes.epfl.ch and follow the instructions in the email response. This email asks to send an empty reply to a given complicated email address containing a hash.

To unsubscribe from the mailing list, send an email to dbtoaster-announce-unsubscribe@listes.epfl.ch from the same email address that you subscribed with.
